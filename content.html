About the Workshop

AI is reshaping the future—not just through technology, but through creative business strategy, design, and smarter ways of working. This interactive session is for business owners, consultants, designers, innovators, and AI specialists who want to harness AI as a creative and strategic enabler in their organizations.

  • Explore what’s possible at the intersection of human-centered AI and intentional design.
  • Learn frameworks and real-world tools to drive innovation and productivity.
  • Future-proof your skills in an evolving digital world.
